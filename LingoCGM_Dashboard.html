Glycemic Variability Metrics KEY INDICATORS
GMI
—
%
Glucose Management Indicator. CGM-derived HbA1c estimate. Target <7.0% for T2D on metformin.
CV
—
%
Coefficient of Variation (SD÷mean). <36% = stable control.
Std Deviation
—
mg/dL
Spread of glucose values. Lower = more consistent control.
MAGE
—
mg/dL
Mean Amplitude of Glycemic Excursions. Target <140 mg/dL in treated T2D.
Fasting Glucose
—
mg/dL avg
Average 6–10 AM readings (fasting proxy). Key metformin efficacy marker.
Nocturnal Avg
—
mg/dL (0–4 AM)
Overnight glucose mean. Elevated values indicate hepatic insulin resistance.
